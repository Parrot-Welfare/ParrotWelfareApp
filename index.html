Parrot Welfare Assessment

After filling out this helpful questionnaire, you will find out how good your pet parrots' life really is. This test can be customised depending on your parrots' species and calculates your score representing how good your bird's care truly is. Afterwards, you will receive tips on how best to improve your pet's quality of life.

About Us

This site was created out of a love for these often mistreated animals, as we think all creatures deserve a happy and fulfilling life free of stress and pain. Please think about what you are getting into when adopting a pet, especially one with such complex needs. So many innocent animals suffer every day at the hands of neglectful and abusive humans, our team's goal is to spread awareness and help inform people so parrots worldwide get to live happier lives.
